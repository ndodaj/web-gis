Data Approval
- "Data Approval" is a methodical process ensuring data accuracy and reliability.
- It involves reviewing a list of data approvals, encompassing various types of information.
- Users approve data by selecting an indicator
Indicator Managemnent
- "Indicator Management" is a systematic approach to organizing and controlling data indicators/layers
-It involves overseeing a variety of indicator, such as in geospatial systems or software.
- Users can add, edit, delete, or approve indicator categories, indicators and attributes adjusting settings and visibility as needed.
- This process streamlines data presentation, enhances usability, and ensures accuracy for efficient analysis.
User Managemnent
- "User Management" is a systematic approach to organizing and controlling data layers
- It involves overseeing a variety of layers, such as in geospatial systems or software.
- Users can add, edit, delete, or approve indicator categories, indicators and attributes adjusting settings and visibility as needed.
- This process streamlines data presentation, enhances usability, and ensures accuracy for efficient analysis.
History
- "History" tracks the chronology of data approval processes.
- It encompasses the recording and archiving of all data approval activities.
- Users can review past approvals, including dates, changes made, and responsible parties.
- This comprehensive record-keeping ensures transparency, accountability, and regulatory compliance.
Document
- "Document Management" is a structured approach to organizing and controlling documents.
- Users can upload, view, edit, and downloads documents within the system.
